IT'S FUN!

Welcome to my fun page, a space where I’ve gathered a collection of my creative work that lives outside the realm of graphic design. Here, you’ll find projects, experiments, and personal pieces that reflect my interests, ideas, and explorations beyond my primary discipline.


This body of work brings together a range of media, including photography, painting, drawing, and printmaking. The photographic pieces investigate the qualities of light, focusing on its ability to shape perception and atmosphere. In printmaking, I explore hybridity and folklore through a chimera combining a ram, puffin, and pangolin, alongside an intaglio aquatint informed by the legend of the Pukwudgie. The collection is rounded out by three paintings and a drawing that captures the energy and movement of a figure performing the worm.
