E. A. Poe

E. A. Poe Publishing is a conceptual publishing house imagining Edgar Allan Poe in the present day, where his signature themes of mystery, suspense, and the macabre are reinterpreted through a modern publishing lens.

Design System

Logo

The E. A. Poe Publishing logo takes inspiration from Edgar Allan Poe’s original occupation, reimagining aspects of his early working life into a symbolic visual identity that connects his historical background with the conceptual world of the publishing house.


Typography

The font that was used for the creation of this brand was Libre Bondoni in bold.


Business Stationery

The business stationery for E. A. Poe Publishing creates a cohesive extension of the brand identity. The envelope features a modified version of the logo, drawing inspiration from carrier pigeons as a symbolic reference to historic forms of communication and message transmission.
